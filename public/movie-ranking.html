Justin's Top 3 Movies

These are Justin Joon Kim's top three favorite movies of all time.

Finding Nemo

This is my favorite movie of all time because I am extremely touched by the masterpiece of a story
that this movie presents.

Rush Hour

Absolutely hilarious action movie that was far ahead of its time in content!

Stand and Deliver

Stand and Deliver is a bit more of a niche movie to watch.
The reason why this movie stands in the top 3 above the rest is because of the
profound message that it sends about hardship, perseverance, and faith.
